Birthday Countdown

Using Python, this program recieves and formats the user's input, then calculates the user's age and time until the user's birthday. The program then displays a real-time countdown until the user's birthday.

Every year deserves a celebration. Are you ready for your birthday? Execute the program below to begin your countdown timer!
