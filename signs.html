Astrological Signs

Using Python, this program recieves and formats the user's input, then determines which astrological sign is associated with the user's birthday. The program then displays the user's astrological sign.

Astrological signs make up the twelve 30 degree sections within Earth's 360 degree orbit around the Sun. Every date on the calandar has a unique astrological sign associated with it. Execute the program below to find your sign!
